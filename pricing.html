Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No. Popy runs locally on your Mac. Your notes never leave your device unless you choose to back them up.

What happens after my trial ends?

You keep full access to all your existing notes and data. AI features require a subscription to continue using.

Can I restore a purchase on a new device?

Yes. Use the "Restore a purchase" option during setup with your billing email to verify and restore your subscription.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support if you're not satisfied.

Do you support invite codes?
